RESILIENCE

Welcome to Resilience! We all have difficulties, trials, and tribulations. Resilience allows people to talk about the times they triumphed and overcame these troubles, giving us hope when we fall. It is meant for those who either overcame or are working to overcome a challenge to discuss them. It is also for those who are currently working to make it through life's challenges to discover that they are conquerable and how they are able to be overcome.

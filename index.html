RESILIENCE

we're human . . .

It's tiring sometimes. Feeling like you can't do it, feeling like it's too hard. It seems overwhelming and sometimes not worth it. But there are also times you triumph, problems you get through, things you overcome. Resilience allows you to talk about the challenges you overcame, whether that be doing well in a class, finding a new career path, or discovering your passion. It is a place where people who have found success can discuss their challenges and winnings. And it is also a place for those who are working toward success to find hope and know that achievement is possible.
